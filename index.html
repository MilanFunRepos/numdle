Dead and Wounded (Numdle)

Welcome to Dead and Wounded. The rules are simple, the computer generates a number and you have to guess what that number is
But that could take forever!
That's where the dead and wounded come in. If you guess an integer thats in the right position, that integer would be considered dead and if you guess a correct integer but in the wrong position, it is wounded.

Example: If the computer generates 1234 and
  • You make a guess of 1235, that would be 3 dead and 0 wounded. (3 integers in the right positions and one integer thats not in the number to be guessed)
  • A guess of 1243 woud be 2 dead and 2 wounded (All 4 integers are present, two in the right positions and two in the wrong positions)
  • And a guess of 5678 would be 0 dead and 0 wounded (No correct integer)
